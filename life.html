AUTOBIOGRAPHY

I am Kristine Soroten, a 20 years old lady born and living in Mapandan, Pangasinan. I am the second born of three and the only daughter of Romeo and Judith Soroten. Currently taking a Bachelor's degree in Pangasinan State University-Urdaneta City Campus.

When I was a kid, I wanted to be a teacher. When I entered high school, i've got really confused about what degree I should take. There are times that I wanted to take computer science, mass communication and psychology and I even imagine myself entering a military academy but having thought about it now, none of those came true. Taking up a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology never crossed my mind but i'm a second year college now. 

I have lots of doubts. The what if's always on my mind everyday. I am always thinking about worst-case scenarios that might happen because of the decision I made that It became my hobby now. If there's someone that will ask me what I really wanted to become, what are my interests in life or what are my dreams; I always found myself having difficulties answering those questions. On top of that, I always choose to become brave. To become brave enough in waiting patiently to the answers for the questions that life will throw to me.
